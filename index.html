Our Story

A Passion 37 years in the making

Born in the rolling hills of Tuscany, La Stellina was founded in 1987 by the Ferretti family on one belief — that food is the most honest expression of love. We started as a small trattoria on Via della Luce, serving the neighbors who became family.

Today our kitchen blends the warmth of an Italian grandmother's table with the precision of two Michelin stars. Every pasta is hand-cut. Every sauce is slow-simmered. Every plate carries the heart of Italy.
